I am an associate professor in the Graphic Communication Department at California Polytechnic State University, in San Luis Obispo. I received my degree of Master of Fine Arts in design with a specialization in college and university teaching from The Ohio State University in the spring of 2012. As a researcher, my focus is on interaction and motion design.

I hold an MBA in marketing from Fundação Getúlio Vargas – FGV (Rio de Janeiro) and a Bachelor of Science in both visual communication and industrial design, from Escola Superior de Desenho Industrial – ESDI (Rio de Janeiro).
